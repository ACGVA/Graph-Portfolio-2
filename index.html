About the course

Generative AI for Work & Research (GRAPH Courses, Q2 2025) is a 10‑week, project‑based programme that equips professionals and researchers with the skills to harness state‑of‑the‑art generative models in their day‑to‑day workflows. Through weekly labs and a capstone project, we experiment with large language models, multimodal generation and autonomous agents to automate knowledge synthesis, create novel content and accelerate decision‑making.

Learning outcomes

  1. Explain the architecture and mechanics of leading generative model families (LLMs, diffusion, transformers).
  2. Design effective prompts and toolchains that integrate generative AI into professional tasks.
  3. Prototype and evaluate an AI‑powered solution to a real research or workplace challenge.
  4. Apply responsible‑AI principles, including bias mitigation, data privacy and transparency.
